Lasagna

Description

A rich and creamy whole-wheat pasta dish filled layer by layer with refreshingly fresh onions and garlic, lathered in a succulent sauce and topped with imported, premium quality mozzarella.

Ingredients

    Garlic Paste
    Lasagna
    Milk
    Cheese
    Pasta Sauce

Steps

  1. Add lasgan to boil and boil it for 10 minutes
  2. Add pasta sauce to pan
  3. After the pasta are boiled put pasta in hot sauce
  4. Serve and Enjoy!
